# raspyradio

## Hardware

- serial LCD based on ST7565
- two buttons
- some LED
- piece of breadboard
- wire
- and soldering skils :)

## Connections

Buttons:
left button to 8
right to 18

LCD:
LCD_CS to 24
LCD_RST to 21
LCD_A0 to 23
LCD_CLK to 22
LCD_SI to 17

LED to 25 GPIO
